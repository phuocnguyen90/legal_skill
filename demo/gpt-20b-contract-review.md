### Draft Red‑Line / Addendum Suggestions

#### 1\. Fees & Payment

> **“Báo giá và Thanh toán”**  
> *Khách hàng* shall pay ABC Law Firm a total fee of **15 000 000 VND** (excluding VAT).  
> *An 80 % deposit* (12 000 000 VND) is payable upon acceptance of this quotation; the deposit is **non‑refundable** only if ABC Law Firm fails to commence the service within **10 days** of the deposit.  
> The remaining **20 %** (3 000 000 VND) is payable upon delivery of the finalized registration documents.  
> All amounts are exclusive of **VAT**; the Client is responsible for paying applicable taxes.

> **Comments** – Clarify that the deposit is *conditional* and not refundable only when the service is not initiated.

* * *

#### 2\. Confidentiality / Non‑Disclosure

> **“Bảo mật Thông tin”**  
> Each Party shall keep confidential all information disclosed by the other, including but not limited to identification documents, corporate plans, and any other non‑public data.  
> The obligations survive the termination of this agreement for **three (3) years**.

> **Comments** – Protects sensitive client data and prevents misuse.

* * *

#### 3\. Indemnification

> **“Bồi thường và Giải quyết Thiệt hại”**  
> ABC Law Firm shall indemnify and hold harmless Khách hàng against all claims, losses, and liabilities arising from ABC Law Firm’s negligence or breach of this agreement.

> **Comments** – Gives the client protection from ABC Law Firm’s mistakes.

* * *

#### 4\. Limitation of Liability

> **“Giới hạn Trách nhiệm”**  
> ABC Law Firm’s total liability under this engagement shall be limited to **80 %** of the fees paid by the Client (excluding VAT).  
> No liability shall arise for indirect, special, or consequential damages.

> **Comments** – Caps damages to protect the firm.

* * *

#### 5\. Term & Termination

> **“Thời hạn và Chấm dứt”**  
> This engagement shall commence on the date of Client’s payment of the deposit and shall continue until the registration is delivered, unless terminated earlier by either Party.  
> Either Party may terminate the engagement by giving **30 days’ written notice** to the other.  
> Termination for “lý do quan trọng” (e.g., fraud, violation of law) shall be immediate without notice.  
> Termination does not relieve either Party of obligations accrued prior to termination.

> **Comments** – Provides clarity on how and when the engagement can end.

* * *

#### 6\. Governing Law & Dispute Resolution

> **“Điều chỉnh hợp đồng và Giải quyết Tranh chấp”**  
> This agreement shall be governed by the laws of **Vietnam**.  
> Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this agreement shall first be submitted to **mediation**.  
> If mediation fails, the dispute shall be settled by **arbitration** under the Rules of the Hanoi International Arbitration Center (HIAC).

> **Comments** – Establishes jurisdiction and a structured dispute‑resolution pathway.

* * *

#### 7\. Assignment

> **“Chuyển nhượng”**  
> Neither Party may assign or delegate its rights or obligations without the prior written consent of the other, which shall not be unreasonably withheld.

* * *

#### 8\. Notices

> **“Thông báo”**  
> All notices or communications required or permitted under this agreement shall be in writing and delivered by certified mail, courier, or electronic mail to the addresses listed above.

* * *

#### 9\. Data Protection

> **“Bảo vệ Dữ liệu”**  
> Both Parties shall process personal data only to the extent necessary to perform the services and shall comply with the Vietnamese Personal Data Protection Law.  
> ABC Law Firm shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal information from unauthorized access.

* * *

#### 10\. Entire Agreement / Entirety

> **“Toàn bộ Thỏa thuận”**  
> This engagement letter, together with any attachments and referenced documents, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties.  
> No other representations, warranties, or agreements shall be binding unless set forth in a written document signed by both Parties.
